League of Legends Roles

Discover the core roles that define the Rift, with iconic champions representing each playstyle.

Top Lane

The Top Lane is home to durable champions that thrive in solo duels and tanky frontlines.

Darius

A juggernaut who dominates 1v1s with bleed effects and powerful dunk executions.

Shen

A global tank who shields allies and teleports across the map to protect teammates.

Jungle

Junglers control neutral objectives, gank lanes, and dictate the tempo of the game.

Lee Sin

A mobile fighter known for flashy combos, high tempo plays, and early game dominance.

Evelynn

A stealth assassin who hunts the unsuspecting, striking from the shadows.

Mid Lane

Mid laners are versatile carries, excelling at magic damage, assassinations, and map roaming.

Ahri

A mobile mage-assassin with charm and dashes, ideal for picks and skirmishes.

Yasuo

A crit-based swordsman who thrives on mobility, knock-ups, and flashy outplays.

ADC

Attack Damage Carries dish out consistent ranged damage, scaling into late-game threats.

Jinx

A hyper-carry marksman who excels in chaotic fights with rockets and resets.

Ashe

A utility marksman who provides vision control, slows, and global initiations.

Support

Supports protect allies, provide vision, and control fights with utility or engage tools.

Thresh

A versatile support with hooks, lanterns, and peel tools for playmaking.

Sona

A musical enchanter who empowers allies with auras and stuns enemies with her crescendo.
